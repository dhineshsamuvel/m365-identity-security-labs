## Objective
Administer Microsoft Teams collaboration and security settings.

## Environment
Microsoft Teams Admin Center

## Configuration Performed
- Created Teams and channels
- Configured meeting policies
- Managed app permission policies
- Reviewed external access settings

## Security Rationale
Meeting and app controls prevent data leakage and unauthorized collaboration.  
External access settings reduce exposure to untrusted domains.

## Outcome
- Teams collaboration configured
- Meetings and apps governed
- External access controlled

## Validation
Teams behavior reflected configured policies.

## Interview Talking Points
- Meeting policy controls
- App permission governance
- External access vs guest access
